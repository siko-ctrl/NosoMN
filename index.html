Frequently Asked Questions

What uptime do you provide for Masternodes?

Our service offers a 99% uptime, with occasional maintenance or unforeseen issues requiring a brief period of downtime. Rest assured, our team is always on hand to quickly resolve any issues that may arise and ensure that your nodes are up and running smoothly. Thank you for choosing our service.

Where are the masternodes hosted?

Our service is proud to offer multiple hosting locations across the United States, including Chicago, Illinois; Atlanta, Georgia; San Jose, California; Ashburn, Virginia; and Dallas, Texas. This allows for greater accessibility and convenience for our clients. Thank you for choosing our service.

Do i have to send any Noso to the service?

You do not need to send us any Nosocoins at all. All you need to do is send us your address so that your coins are completely safe with us. For example: 1.NXC3XXXXXXX. Thank you for choosing our service.

What are the specs of the MN?

Our hosting service boasts the following impressive specifications: 1x Xeon vcore, 768 MB RAM, 1 TB of bandwidth, SSD Raid 10, and availability in 5 different geographic locations. Our top-of-the-line infrastructure ensures that your nodes will be able to run smoothly and efficiently.

How can I book a Masternode?

If you would like to join our Discord server and contact the owner, Siko, for more information about our services, please DM them and they will get back to you as soon as possible. Alternatively, you can email us at teamndrs@gmail.com with your name, email address, MN addresses containing sufficient Noso, the number of MN's you would like us to host, and your preferred payment method. A member of the NosoMN team will be in touch with you shortly after receiving your submission. Thank you for considering our services.
